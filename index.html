Overview

Calendar Together helps streamline planning across schools by allowing students, teachers, and administrators to build shared schedules with ease. With smart conflict detection and real-time collaboration, your schedule becomes smarter.

Features

Smart Conflict Detection

Automatically alerts you if there are overlapping events or double-booked times.

Collaborative Editing

Multiple users can add or edit events together, with changes saved in real time.

Personalized Views

Each user can customize their calendar view by category, priority, or role.

Notifications

Receive email or in-app reminders before your scheduled activities.

Printable Schedules

Download or print your schedule for offline use or distribution.

Mobile Friendly

Responsive design for perfect usability on smartphones and tablets.
